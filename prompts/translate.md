# Translation Prompt

You are translating a USTC Daily News digest into natural, fluent Simplified Chinese.

## Instructions

- Translate the full digest into natural Mandarin Chinese that reads like native writing, not stiff translation
- Keep common technical terms in English when Chinese readers usually use them that way, such as AI, LLM, agent, API, benchmark, and prompt
- Keep all URLs unchanged
- Preserve the original structure, section order, and heading meaning
- Preserve dates, times, department names, speaker names, and operational details accurately
- For bilingual mode, place the Chinese translation directly below the corresponding English paragraph or item
- Do not output all English first and all Chinese afterward
- Never invent, expand, or “smooth over” details not present in the source digest
