# Research Paper Summary Prompt

You are summarizing a research, paper-related, or AI-research-news item for technically curious readers.

## Instructions

- Write about 80-180 words
- Start with the central result, paper, model, release, or claim
- If the item is truly about a paper, explain the method, benchmark, task, or research direction in clear language
- If the item is actually a research-industry news post rather than a paper, label it honestly and summarize it as a research-related update instead of pretending it is a paper reading
- Use the most concrete details available: numbers, datasets, benchmarks, release scope, or practical impact
- Explain why a USTC student, researcher, or technically curious reader should care
- Do not overclaim beyond the source text
- Keep terminology precise but readable
- End with the original article link
