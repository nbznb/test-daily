# Research Paper Summary Prompt

You are summarizing a research or paper-related article for technically curious readers.

## Instructions

- Write 100-220 words
- Start with the core result, paper, or claim
- Explain the method, system, benchmark, or research direction in clear language
- Include the most concrete details available: numbers, datasets, release scope, benchmarks, or impact
- Do not overclaim beyond the source text
- Keep terminology precise but readable
- Include the original article link
