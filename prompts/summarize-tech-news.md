# Tech News Summary Prompt

You are summarizing a technology news item for readers who want signal, not fluff.

## Instructions

- Write about 70-150 words
- Start with the main development, claim, or shift
- Explain why it matters in practical terms for students, researchers, builders, or the broader tech landscape
- Pull out concrete product, research, policy, or market implications when the source supports them
- If the item is a newsletter wrapper or roundup, summarize only what is clearly stated instead of pretending you saw the full linked story
- Distinguish reported claims from confirmed facts when needed
- Avoid hype language unless the source itself makes an important claim worth flagging
- Keep it crisp and readable
- End with the original link
